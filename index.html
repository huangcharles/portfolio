About this site

This web page was created as a digital portfolio for Charlie Huang's work in Advanced Computer Science, completed concurrently at Rosemount High School and Inver Hills Community College in the 2019-20 AY, taught by Professor Dr. Thomas Reinartz, Ph.D. The site's Khemistry Fun! tab features photos, memes, and haikus relating to AP Khemistry, a term of endearment given by the students of Ms. Kendra Olsen's 2017-18 AP Chemistry class at Rosemount High School.

Rosemount High School in Rosemount, MN, is a public high school serving approximately 2,200 9-12 graders. Rosemount High School is the largest of 11 secondary schools in Independent School District 196, the 4th largest school district in the state of Minnesota.
