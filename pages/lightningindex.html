Problem 1: Starting position of lighting along rim of Van de Graaff Generator

Used equation of a circle and solved for x in terms of y. Generated random y value between the domain of y values. Substituted random y value into equation of circle to get x.
(x-136)^2+(y-390)^2=110^2
Solving for x:
x=136+sqrt(-y2+780y-140000)
The code ends up being: startX=136+Math.sqrt(-1*Math.pow(startY, 2)+780*startY-140000);
